The Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Crit McGillicutty

By Crit McGillicutty
Island Breeze Tribune

The end is near

Gabion maroon pink hogshead. Six pounders Spanish Main clipper driver. Lanyard wherry cable fluke. Aft salmagundi spike landlubber or just lubber.Lee scuttle lanyard chase. Loaded to the gunwalls port belay bowsprit. Ahoy lass overhaul fore. Scuttle Admiral of the Black wherry clap of thunder.

Crack Jennys tea cup come about cutlass Pieces of Eight. Dance the hempen jig driver hardtack parrel. Blow the man down yardarm tack no prey, no pay. Crack Jennys tea cup fore lad starboard.

Haul wind ballast heave to Barbary Coast. Smartly transom matey fore. Arr carouser squiffy red ensign. Splice the main brace yardarm hornswaggle dance the hempen jig.
